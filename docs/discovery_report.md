## 3. 🔥 Real Customer Pain Points (From Reviews)

*Sourced from G2, Capterra, SoftwareSuggest, Techjockey, Reddit, Indian forums*

### 🔴 CRITICAL — Universally Complained About

| # | Pain Point | Who Says This | What They Say |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Pricing too high / hidden fees** | Small salon owners everywhere | "Fresha promised free forever — now they charge 20% on every new client I bring in myself via Instagram" |
| 2 | **Too complex to use** | Receptionists & owners | "Zenoti is NASA-level complex for a 5-staff salon. 5 clicks to add a walk-in." |
| 3 | **No WhatsApp integration** | Indian market (all products) | "SMS goes to spam. My clients only respond on WhatsApp. Why can't you send the invoice on WhatsApp?" |
| 4 | **Staff commission tracking is wrong** | Owners using MioSalon, Salonist | "The software can't handle tiered commissions. I still do it in Excel every month-end." |
| 5 | **GST billing non-compliant** | Indian market (global tools) | "No CGST/SGST split, no HSN codes. I can't file returns with this invoice format." |

### 🟠 HIGH — Frequently Cited

| # | Pain Point | Insight |
|---|---|---|
| 6 | **Poor support during salon hours** | Support is 9-6 weekdays; salons are busiest on evenings and weekends |
| 7 | **Loyalty programs are passive and broken** | Clients don't know their points balance; staff forget to apply them |
| 8 | **Slow, complex online booking** | 71% booking abandonment rate; clients give up mid-flow |
| 9 | **System crashes on peak days** | Saturday mornings are busiest — also when systems go down most |
| 10 | **Data lock-in / can't export** | "I want to leave but I can't take 3 years of client history with me" |

### 🟡 INDIA-SPECIFIC — Uniquely Unaddressed

| Pain Point | Detail |
|---|---|
| **UPI/QR payments** | 70%+ of India salon payments are UPI; most software doesn't track it |
| **Walk-in heavy model** | Indian salons have 40–60% walk-ins; software is appointment-first → awkward for walk-ins |
| **Package/combo billing** | "10-session facial package" tracking across visits is poorly done |
| **Staff salary advance tracking** | Common in India; never supported in software |
| **Festival / seasonal promos** | Diwali, wedding season — no quick campaign tools |
| **Referral tracking** | Word-of-mouth is #1 Indian salon acquisition channel; "referred by" isn't tracked |
| **Hindi/regional language UI** | Staff in Tier 2/3 cities aren't comfortable in English-only tools |

## 5. ✅ Revised MVP Feature List

### 🔴 Must Have (MVP V1)

| Feature | Why |
|---|---|
| Customer profiles + visit history | Core CRM — our primary value prop |
| Staff / professional profiles | Foundation for tracking and billing |
| Service catalog with pricing | Needed for billing and performance data |
| Create visit → add services → tag professional | The core workflow — connective tissue of the product |
| **GST-compliant invoice generation** | Non-negotiable for Indian market |
| **UPI + Cash + Card payment tracking** | 70% of India salon payments are UPI |
| **Invoice sharing via WhatsApp** | How Indian customers expect to receive bills |
| Staff performance view (by day / period) | Core value prop for owner |
| Customer visit frequency report | Core value prop for customer retention |
| Owner dashboard (revenue, top services, top staff) | Owner needs a daily summary |

### 🟠 High Priority (MVP V1.5 — shortly after launch)

| Feature | Why |
|---|---|
| Commission calculation per professional | Month-end disputes are a top pain point |
| End-of-day cash reconciliation view | Cash vs UPI vs card split |
| Customer re-engagement list (lapsed customers) | "Who hasn't visited in 30+ days?" |
| Basic package / combo bill tracking | Indian salons heavily sell packages |
| Invoice PDF download | Backup for WhatsApp share |

### 🟡 Nice to Have (V2)

| Feature | Why |
|---|---|
| Appointment / booking calendar | Walk-in first, but bookings will come |
| Staff leaderboard / gamification | Retention and adoption driver |
| AI churn prediction | Differentiation in V2 |
| Inventory management | Separate module |
| WhatsApp automated reminders | Upgrade from manual share |
| Loyalty / membership programs | Retention tool |

## 6. 🎯 Strategic Positioning

### Where We Win

> **"The fastest walk-in billing tool built for Indian salon owners — GST-ready, UPI-native, and so simple your receptionist can learn it in 10 minutes."**

### Our Unfair Advantages
1. **India-first from day one** — GST, UPI, WhatsApp — not afterthoughts
2. **Billing speed** — Optimised for the counter moment; faster than any existing tool
3. **No appointment-first bias** — Built for the reality of walk-in heavy Indian salons
4. **Fair pricing** — No per-user fees, no commissions on your own clients
5. **Simplicity** — No NASA-level complexity; only features that matter for a 5-staff salon

### Our Biggest Competitor to Watch
> **DINGG** — They have WhatsApp API, UPI, GST, and AI. They are the closest to this vision. We need to research their pricing and UX weaknesses closely before building.

## 7. ❓ Open Questions for User Decision

1. **Commission structure:** Should V1 include commission tracking? It's a high-pain point but adds complexity.
2. **WhatsApp invoice sharing:** Manual share (copy link) or native WhatsApp Business API integration in V1?
3. **GST:** Do we generate GST-compliant invoices from day one, even if it adds dev complexity?
4. **Pricing model:** Flat monthly fee? Per-user? Freemium?
5. **Target city tier:** Tier 1 (Mumbai, Bangalore) first or Tier 2/3 (Pune, Jaipur, Surat)?
